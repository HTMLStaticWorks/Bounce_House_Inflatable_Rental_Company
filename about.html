Born from a Love of Play

Founded in 2015, JumpJoy started as a small family business with just three bounce houses. Today, we are the region's leading provider of inflatable entertainment, serving over 1,000 events annually.

Our mission is simple: To provide safe, clean, and ridiculously fun entertainment that makes every child's party unforgettable.
